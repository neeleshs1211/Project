frequently asked questions

how to cancel?

Visit our website, log in, go to "My Bookings," and select the reservation to cancel. For help, contact customer service.

is there any vacancy?

Check room availability using our online booking tool or call the front desk for updates.

what are payment methods?

We accept major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), debit cards, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. Contact billing for special arrangements.

how to claim coupons code?

Enter the coupon code during online booking. If valid, the discount applies. For issues, contact support.

what are the age requirements?

Guests must be 18+ to book. Minors must be with a parent or guardian. Additional age restrictions may apply to certain facilities.
